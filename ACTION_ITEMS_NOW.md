### 3. Google Search Console Submission (15 minutes)

**Step 1: Create Account**
1. Go to [https://search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Click "Start now"
3. Sign in with your Google account (create one if needed)

**Step 2: Add Property**
1. Click "Add property" or "Create property"
2. Select "Domain" tab
3. Enter your domain: `ravishankar-portfolio.com` (without www or https://)
4. Click "Continue"

**Step 3: Verify Ownership**
Choose **DNS verification** (easiest):
1. Google will show you a TXT record to add
2. Copy the entire TXT record
3. Go to your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Google Domains, etc.)
4. Find DNS settings
5. Add the TXT record
6. Return to Google Search Console
7. Click "Verify"
8. Wait 2-3 minutes, then refresh

**Alternative: HTML File Upload**
1. Download the HTML verification file Google provides
2. Upload to root of your website: `ravishankar-portfolio.com/verification-file.html`
3. Click "Verify" in Google Search Console

**Step 4: Submit Sitemap**
1. In Google Search Console, find "Sitemaps" in left menu
2. Click "Add/Test Sitemap"
3. Enter: `ravishankar-portfolio.com/sitemap.xml`
4. Click "Submit"
5. Google will start processing

**Step 5: Request Indexation**
1. Go to "URL Inspection" tool
2. Enter your homepage: `ravishankar-portfolio.com`
3. Click "Request indexation"
4. Wait 24 hours for indexation
